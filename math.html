About

Dice is a website designed to teach casino mathematics through easy to follow articles. It's intended to get you to understand the underlying mathematical principles behind gambling, by closely examining a few of the most popular 'games'. I will also try to include a history of the games and some anecdotes to contextualise them a bit. I hope you can take away something useful from here.

P.S. I do NOT encourage or promote any form of gambling. Do it at your own risk. :)
